# Research Domain: Market Landscape

## Scope
Competitive analysis, market sizing, and positioning for institutional event derivatives.

## Key Hypotheses
1. TAM for institutional event risk > $10B globally
2. Expert networks (AlphaSights, GLG) are the primary competition
3. Polymarket/Kalshi prove product-market fit but lack institutional features
4. Pricing power exists at $50K-500K/year per institutional client
