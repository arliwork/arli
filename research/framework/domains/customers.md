# Research Domain: Customer Intelligence

## Scope
Ideal customer profiles, buying processes, pain points, and procurement for institutional event risk products.

## Key Hypotheses
1. Chief Risk Officer (CRO) is the economic buyer at hedge funds
2. Family offices buy through consultants (gatekeeper model)
3. Compliance cost reduction is the #1 value driver
4. Pilot programs convert at 60%+ to annual contracts

## Success Criteria
- 3 detailed ICPs with org charts
- Procurement process mapped for each segment
- Pain point prioritization matrix
- Pilot-to-contract conversion benchmarks
