# Research Domain: Business Model & Economics

## Scope
Revenue models, pricing strategy, unit economics, and partnership structures for ARLI.

## Key Hypotheses
1. Hybrid subscription + transaction fee maximizes revenue
2. $100K ACV achievable for top-tier hedge funds
3. Market maker partnerships reduce capital requirements by 80%
4. Break-even at 50 institutional clients

- 3 pricing tiers defined with rationale
- Unit economics model (CAC, LTV, payback)
- Partnership framework documented
- Break-even analysis by scenario
